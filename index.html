From Analysis to Design

Part 1: Mapping Pain Points to Usability Heuristics

Label each pain point with a usability heuristic. Write a design brief for each issue.

Part 2: Design Briefs

Design Brief 1
Issue When working on a brief, the "Brief Saved" button is always greyed out. This will cause confusion as it doesn't give confirmation that your work has saved recently.(H1 Visability of System Status)
Brief Make the "Brief Saved" button highlighted and able to be clicked again to save or by having it show that it is constantly saving by saying "Saving" as the user types then switching back to "Saved".
Design Brief 2
Issue After creating your brief, you need to click back into "All Briefs" to find if your brief was even created. You must then scroll down until you recognize your new brief.(H1 Visability of System Status)
Brief Make the "My Work" tab at the top of the screen show your new briefs or, in the "All Briefs" tab, have your briefs all show at the top of the page. Mine was all the way at the bottom.
Design Brief 3
Issue The "Create New Brief" button is quite small and easy to miss for new users. (H6 Recognition over Recall)
Brief Make the "Create New Brief" button larger or have the user create new briefs in the "My Work" tab instead of "All Briefs" as it would help with overall confusion.
